Yoga Asanas

Downward Dog (Adho Mukha Svanasana)

Strengthens the arms and legs, stretches the back, and increases flexibility. Calorie Burn: ~50 calories/10 mins.

Warrior Pose (Virabhadrasana)

Improves balance, stability, and strength. Calorie Burn: ~45 calories/10 mins.

Tree Pose (Vrikshasana)

Enhances concentration, balance, and stability. Calorie Burn: ~30 calories/10 mins.

Pranayams

Breath of Fire (Kapalbhati)

Helps in detoxification, enhances lung capacity, and increases metabolism.

Alternate Nostril Breathing (Nadi Shodhana)

Balances the nervous system, relieves stress, and improves focus.

Lion's Breath (Simhasana)

Releases tension, stimulates the throat, and relieves stress.

Face Yoga

Cheek Lifter

Tones cheek muscles and reduces sagging skin.

Fish Face

Strengthens cheek and jaw muscles, helps reduce fat.

Forehead Smoothener

Reduces wrinkles and relaxes forehead muscles.
